Canine Quest

About the Game

In Canine Quest, two puppy pals have been separated from their owners in the woods. The woods is a scary place full of different challenges the two must over come. Bella and Luna must combine their unique puppy skills in order to escape the woods and return to their loving owners!

Bella

Bella is a tiny, but fiesty pup. She's got the agility and energy to move through small spaces, and even utilize Luna to navigate the forrest!

Abilities & Interactions

Bella can already jump super high. But with Luna's help, Bella can climb ontop of her and get a boost! (Hint: With Bella ontop of Luna, try jumping with both dogs at the same time!)

Vines blocking your path? Bella will eat through any vines in front of her.

Luna

Luna is a wise, fluffy dog who uses her strength and smarts to navigate the woods. Nothing blocks her path! But Luna can't do everything herself.

Abilities & Interactions

Luna can dig through ground to uncover secret passage ways (or even hidden treats).

Luna can use her strength to push boulders out of the way.
